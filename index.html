Los Angeles County Traffic Collisions Analysis

EXPLORE BELOW

On Average 3,287 people die each DAY just from car collisions.

Men participate in more collisions than females in LA County

Traffic collisions are one of the leading causes of death in the United States.

For this reason traffic collisions are a great candidate for study. By analyzing different factors involved in traffic collisions, we may unveil invaluable insights about these occurances. We chose to analyze traffic collissions in Los Angeles County (LA), as it is a large commuter city.

Gender Analysis



Herein it was found that males presented a higher occurance of traffic collisions than females in Los Angeles County.

Age Analysis
